Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 27, 2025

AxelBase – Bulk Barcode SVG Generator is a fully client-side web application. This means your data never leaves your device — not even for a millisecond.

We Collect Nothing

We do not collect, store, log, or transmit:

  • Barcode values or CSV content
  • IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or location data
  • Usage analytics, heatmaps, or session recordings
  • Cookies, localStorage persistence, or tracking pixels

All barcode generation, SVG rendering, and ZIP creation happen exclusively in your browser using JavaScript.

How Your Data Is Handled

When you use AxelBase:

  • Manual entries and CSV uploads are processed in memory
  • bwip-js and JSZip run locally from static assets
  • Generated SVGs and ZIP files are created on-the-fly and offered via Blob URLs
  • Nothing is uploaded to any server — ever

No Third-Party Services

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, Cloudflare Insights, or any external logging. The only external resources are optional CDN-hosted Bootstrap CSS/JS (which collect no personal data).

Security & Responsibility

Because no data reaches our servers, we cannot be breached, subpoenaed, or hacked for your information. You retain full control and responsibility for the data you input and the files you download.

Children’s Privacy

AxelBase does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The tool is intended for general use in business and manufacturing contexts.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates will appear on this page with a revised effective date.

Your privacy is not a feature — it’s the foundation. AxelBase was built for professionals who demand absolute data sovereignty.
